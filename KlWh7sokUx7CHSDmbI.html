Obesity and bronchial asthma – is the link obvious?

Why there is such an addiction, what it is dangerous for health and how to avoid complications – we will figure out today.

Sad tandem

To each of our readers for sure it is known that excess body weight increases the risk of many diseases, being a serious complicating factor. This includes, for example, diabetes mellitus, hypertension, and diseases of the female and male reproductive systems.

But here is the fact that obesity is a provoking factor for the development of pulmonary diseases pathologies are not known to many. Whereas the relationship between excess weight and bronchial asthma was established in the middle of the IX century and was widely described not only by scientists and doctors of that time, but also by writers.

Both diseases are currently very common in developed countries and cause serious health problems fears of the world medical community.

Moreover, the age of patients with them is becoming less and less. No one is surprised by children with an increased body mass index of 25-40%, while for the child's body this is a serious load.

The number of patients with asthma is also growing, including in the younger age group group. Moreover, the number of people with these two diseases at the same time is also increasing. But does asthma provoke obesity, due to the low mobility of patients, or Vice versa?

After conducting a lot of research, scientists have found that people who are prone to excess weight and have it, much more often suffer from bronchial asthma. Withal the course of the disease in them is more severe, and the attacks are more frequent and acute, especially at an early age and among women. In the latter, a set of ailments is diagnosed more often and passes on the background of changes in the hormonal background.

It is scientifically proven that obesity seriously reduces the quality of life, is an aggravating factor development of pulmonary pathologies, dictates their severity and worsens the control of asthma.

What should I do?

But is it possible to avoid complications? Of course, Yes! Since excess weight is provoking, it is important to take control of it. And losing weight is much easier than curing asthma. Plus normalization of body weight it will reduce the risks of other chronic diseases, improve your overall well-being, and fill you with vigor and strength.

Whether you lose weight on your own or seek medical help depends on the individual. For some people, a good motivation is enough, and for others, a complete lack of willpower is easier to compensate with the help of carefully designed weight loss program.

We warn you about mandatory consultation with a medical specialist!
