I lost 32 kg without dieting and training. The real story of an ordinary girl Anna
Hello, we are in touch with "Yamdiet"!

In this article, we will consider the method of losing weight for Anna, a girl who was able to lose 32 kg. She didn't go on hard diets or exercise in the gym.

How did she manage to lose a significant amount of weight?
And at what point did she realize that it was time to take up her health?

About 6 years ago the girl's weight reached the level of 105 kg. Then she faced a number of problems: shortness of breath, poor choice of clothes, incipient diabetes. At that moment, Anna decided that it was time to change something and chose a standard trajectory of weight loss for herself: diet + gym.

Also, the girl severely limited the consumption of fat, flour, salt and fried; she led an active lifestyle.

However, this method was not effective for Anna, as she often had breakdowns (once in three months). Despite such pitfalls, the girl lost 17 kg. Accordingly, a number of problems have been resolved.

A surge of motivation

What really convinced you how to start radical changes-book "The brain against excess weight ", by Daniel Amen. From there, Anna learned that she suffers from compulsive-impulsive overeating (stress is the culprit of eternal hunger).

After reading this book, the girl decided to pass test. She resolutely avoided situations where she could "pick up" negative emotions: she stopped comparing herself with others, did not go to entertainment centers, was not surrounded by food.

And such restrictions gave a positive result â€“ another 15 kg was dropped.

Does the girl support it diet now?

At the moment, Anna listens to her inner desires and tries to find harmony with her body. There are no limits on her diet.

She made a mental note that if she really wanted to eat something, she would allow herself to eat it . However, once it starts to get greedy eating (for example, cookies) will ask yourself: do I want to continue? What good will it do me? The response comes immediately.

The girl's weight for 2020 is 72 kg. It does not stop there and is going to fall below 60 kg. Anna is sure that the most terrible and difficult thing has already passed.

Want to say that the main weapon against fighting excess weight is your internal attitudes and sense of purpose .

Do you have a goal? Then what is stopping you from getting off the path? Imagine what your state will be like when you succeed. Perhaps you will begin to think otherwise.

Ask yourself the question: is it worth it?
