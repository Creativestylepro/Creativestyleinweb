TOP 5 products for beautiful skin, a slim figure and healthy hair cheaper than 100₽

Usually, products for beauty and health of the skin and hair are quite expensive products, such as trout, avocado and quinoa - not everyone can afford them.

In today's publication, we will discuss the TOP 5 products for beautiful skin, a slim figure and healthy hair, which everyone can afford.

1. Meat, liver and offal

Meat and especially liver are not only rich in protein - they also contain a large amount of vitamin a PP that actively participates in cellular metabolism. If you have ever flaked your skin, and your hair has become dull and brittle - it is quite possible that it is vitamin PP that your body lacks.

Other foods rich in vitamin PP are meat offal (kidneys, heart), as well as mushrooms and beer yeast.

2. Vegetables green and orange colors

Beta-carotene (also known as provitamin A) is a powerful natural antioxidant that promotes the regeneration and renewal of skin cells. Remember that group a vitamins are fat-soluble, therefore, for good assimilation, it is better to eat them with butter or other products rich in useful fats.

Lack of vitamin A in the body can cause dry and dehydrated skin on the face and lips, as well as the appearance of premature wrinkles.

3. Citrus fruits and other foods rich in vitamin C C

The citrus family (oranges, tangerines, etc.), as well as bell peppers, tomatoes and sauerkraut contain a large amount of vitamin a C - one of the most powerful powerful antioxidants responsible for healthy skin. ascorbic acid it also strengthens the walls of blood vessels and actively participates in the synthesis of collagen.

A lack of this vitamin can lead to increased fatigue, a dull complexion, flabby and dry skin, and long healing of post-acne and age spots.

These products must be used in the menu, but excessive consumption of them is fraught with allergic reactions.

4. Low-fat dairy products

Low-fat dairy products, as well as low-fat dairy products, will not only not harm your figure, but also they will provide the body with vitamin E and selenium, which not only actively prevent the aging process of the skin by fighting free radicals, but also saturate the cells with vitamins.

Selenium is especially useful for pregnant women and smokers.

5. Bananas

Beloved by many, exotic fruits not only have a positive effect on digestion and sleep, but also contain Biotin (vitamin H, or B7) - one of the main vitamins in the fight for beautiful and healthy skin, hair and nails.
