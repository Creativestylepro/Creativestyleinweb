Clean water will help you lose weight and get your body in order

It is clear that now sitting at home, many people have a problem with gaining excess weight. And this is not surprising, physical activity has decreased dramatically, and please yourself and your friends households with various Goodies increased. Yes that to speak itself such. I will not talk at all about the dangers of excess weight, but will simply give you some tips on how to get rid of extra pounds by drinking enough water.

This need to know

  • A person is one-third water. Almost all organs, starting from the brain and ending with the skin, are more or less composed of fluid. Therefore, it is simply vital to regularly replenish the body's water supply.
  • Water plays a major role in metabolism, than the more you drink it, the faster the metabolic process takes place. Drunk water fills the stomach, respectively, the appetite decreases, and the portion of food eaten becomes smaller. At zero calories, water does not allow fats to be absorbed by the body and harm our forms.
  • The feeling of hunger does not always mean that it is necessary to eat, sometimes it is enough to drink a glass of water to extinguish this desire.
  • How to lose weight with water in a short time
  • A few rules that will help you lose weight quickly and correctly:
  • Every morning, before Breakfast, drink a glass of clean water, diluted with a teaspoon of honey or add lemon juice.
  • Drink a glass of water twenty minutes before each meal. Liquid fills the stomach. Accordingly, you consume less food during meals.
  • The main proportion of water should be drunk before 16.00 hours. Otherwise, night trips to the toilet are provided for you.
  • Not in never skip a meal. Otherwise, with the "hunger" you can eat much more than you should.
  • The daily food ration is recommended to be divided into five meals (Breakfast, lunch, afternoon snack, dinner). At night, you can drink a glass of kefir or yogurt.
  • Water or green tea, you can drink an hour after eating. During meals, the liquid consumed interferes with the digestion of food, which can affect the body's deposition of fats.
  • During the day, if you feel hungry, drink water.
  • The water should be warm. Cool water can provoke feelings of hunger.
  • Soda, coffee, juices from the store are not desirable for drinking.
  • The amount of water can vary from 1.5-3 liters per day, no more. It all depends on your age and weight.

It is not enough to observe a drinking regime, it is important to eat correctly, so that the result is fast and healthy qualitative.

  • Avoid high-calorie foods (fatty, fried, salted, and smoked foods). It is better to forget about alcohol altogether.
  • Do some exercise. Do not use the Elevator, but rather go up on foot, which will allow you to burn extra calories.

The result of the water diet, you will see, in five days. But do not be deceived - it has left your body, excess water. You should not relax, if you do not follow the basic rules of the water diet, you can quickly return the lost pounds. But with proper nutrition and a healthy lifestyle, you can keep yourself in shape and feel good for a long time healthy.

Reduced weight is not the only plus for the body

  • You can "flutter" like a butterfly because of the acquired sense of lightness after losing extra pounds.
  • Your hair will get a healthy look. A in the complex with cosmetic procedures will make your hair shiny and manageable.
  • Your skin will "say" thank you for its elasticity and firmness.
  • Your internal organs will start working like clockwork. After being released from toxins and slags.

Be healthy!
