What should I eat for Breakfast to lose weight?

What should I eat for Breakfast to lose weight?

Weight loss and diet â€“ when we hear these words, we first think about the rejection of favorite foods, hunger and bad mood. But this should not be so, because weight loss can be carried out without stress and rumbling of the stomach. And affect weight loss Breakfast is available. What it should be, you will find out later!

If you want to lose weight, you are faced with an infinite number of diets and nutrition concepts: lose weight by fasting, increasing weight reduce the amount of fat and reduce the proportion of carbohydrates, by using a strict diet, and so on. It is not so easy to understand what strategy you need to use to lose weight.

We learned the opinion of two nutritionists about what is really important when losing weight. First of all, the good news is: if you want to lose weight, you should not starve .

If you want to lose weight, you should eat Breakfast

"A good Breakfast is very important," says Carmen Breler. Brechler is an ecotrophologist and a member of the scientific Advisory Board of the German society of qualified dietitians and nutrition consultants. "Breakfast is a must, especially for those who want to lose weight. It is best to eat Breakfast before leaving the house, " advises Breler. - If you leave the house full, you protect yourself from the appearance of a strong appetite and temptations. This minimizes the risk of consuming a harmful, high-calorie Breakfast on the go." This is exactly what it is a great danger for the figure.

Helmut Fastle of Slim Concept supports Breler's opinion. Together with Anna Fastl, he runs a company that helps people achieve their dream figure. He also says: "Hunger is very harmful for anyone who wants to lose weight." And a good Breakfast is very important for weight loss.

The best food and Breakfast items

* Oatmeal with yogurt : this should be a low-fat natural yogurt that does not contain sugar in any way. You can add a little fruit to the porridge. If you choose dense natural yogurt, you also bring a lot of benefits to your intestinal flora.

Fall it is recommended to soak oatmeal in water, low-fat cow's milk or vegetable milk. "The feeling of fullness comes when the stomach is full," says Fastl. Swollen oatmeal porridge creates a large volume and, therefore, saturates faster. Like Brewer, he recommends eating natural yogurt. "Fruit yogurt from a supermarket contains lots of sugar. The fruits that are available in it are processed, and they no longer contain any nutrients." If you prefer a warm Breakfast, you can cook porridge instead of soaking the cereal.

* Whole grain bread: "Wholegrain bread with low-fat cream cheese or ham is also good for weight loss," he says Brewer.

Fastl counts: "Unsweetened bread is very good for the body and weight loss." As an addition to it, you can choose curd cheese with a low fat content, curd or low-fat cream cheese, improve the taste with herbs and spices. As an alternative to jam Fastl recommends eating fresh fruit with a little sweetener.

* Smoothies: "Be careful with smoothies," warns Breler. "Fructose is not compatible with weight loss." Excess fruit means excess sugar. "It is better to give preference to vegetables: the ratio of vegetables and fruits should be about 3 to 2."

* Eggs: eggs are also good for Breakfast, especially for those who want to lose weight. "The most useful component of eggs is protein," says Fastl. " we Recommend eating scrambled eggs with spices, made from one whole egg and two proteins."

"Good planning is very important for weight loss," says Breler. Â«Better pre-prepare food that matches your weight loss strategy and carry it to work." Or at least prepare a light snack: for example, whole wheat bread or a banana. "Bananas are great for weight loss," says the nutritionist. " they are rich in potassium and vitamins." They are also good for saturating and maintain blood sugar levels at a stable level.

And don't be afraid of carbohydrates: "Carbohydrates should definitely be included in Breakfast," fastl believes. But as you get closer to night, the amount of carbohydrates should decrease. If you want to lose weight, it is also important to drink a lot of clean water.
