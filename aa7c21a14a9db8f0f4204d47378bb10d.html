The best snacks for weight loss

These snacks are very tasty, the main thing is to include them in your caloric content.

1. Buy any grain curd in natural yogurt.In principle, it is very tasty in itself, but I like to add fruit to it, especially banana, you can also add nuts, granola, muesli and TD.Very nutritious, simple and inexpensive!

2. Buy black whole-grain bread( you can add seeds or nuts), smear on it curd cheese (I advise as simple as possible without additives), you can eat it like this, and you can put avocado on top (cut into pieces or making a puree out of it) or add salmon.It turns out very tasty!

3. for this snack, you will need peanut paste( it can be ordered in an online store or purchased in stores of proper nutrition).We smear it on apples and bananas, cut into pieces.The snack is already ready, but you can add yogurt with Chia seeds to it( they are cheaper in online stores)

4. we will need ready-made hummus, carrots and bell peppers.All cut into pieces and dip in hummus.If there is no hummus, then we will make a sauce from sour cream, dill and lemon juice.It turns out quite interesting snack!

5. Buy ricotta cheese and add any jam, berries, fruits or nuts.Interesting taste is guaranteed for you!

All these recipes I use myself, they are unrealistically delicious, be sure to try to cook something of this.
