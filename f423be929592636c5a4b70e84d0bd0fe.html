Lazy diet according to Bubnovsky's advice for women after 50 years + menu for 3 days

How to lose weight without starving or changing your eating habits? How to become slim and beautiful in the shortest possible time?

Overweight people often ask this question. So, for losing weight, a diet has been developed according to the method Bubnovsky, as a result of which it is possible to lose weight within 2 weeks!

It will take a minimum of effort to achieve the result! The result will not make you wait long, just follow the recommendations below.

Water! Here is the basis of this diet! It is consumed in the amount that is necessary to fill the stomach. In this regard, the scale of appetite decreases and the body is satiated with a small portion.

In as a result of consuming the right amount of drinking water, the desire to consume other beverages (cocktails, sweet sodas, etc.) will disappear. This also leads to the loss of extra pounds.

Above all: this nutrition system is absolutely harmless to the body.

Basic rule:

  • Before eating, you should first drink 400 ml of water and only after half an hour you can eat. The main thing before eating food is not to forget about this.
  • After 2 hours after eating, you need to drink 1 glass of green tea. Not a minute earlier! Otherwise, it will interfere with the natural flow of the digestive process.

If you follow these 2 rules, a stunning result is guaranteed. Water in this case: stimulates the production of gastric juice, accelerates the digestion process, burns fat, and washes out toxins.

This diet for lazy women solves the problem of improper metabolism. Water increases metabolic rate. Also, with the help of this diet, you can lose weight in the shortest possible time while avoiding hunger.

Water can be added berries and fruits, experiment to your taste, very harmonizes with the taste buds-warm water with raspberries and orange.

Menu for 3 days:

You should drink only clean water every day 30 minutes before Breakfast, lunch and dinner, and 20 minutes before lunch.
Refuse to use it: flour, chips, fried dishes.

The first day:

  • Breakfast consists of buckwheat porridge with honey, a second Breakfast of any fruit, such as pears.
  • Lunch consists of borscht and a piece of black bread.
  • For an afternoon snack, classic yogurt with slices of any berries.
  • Dinner consists of 2 boiled eggs and tomato's.

Day two:

  • Breakfast consists of scrambled eggs with greens and tomatoes cooked in the microwave.
  • The second Breakfast consists of one grapefruit. Lunch consists of vegetable soup and one piece of buckwheat bread.
  • Afternoon tea consists of one boiled egg and greeneries.
  • Dinner consists of baked chicken with stewed vegetables.

Day three:

  • Breakfast consists of oatmeal porridge with the addition of any berries and honey.
  • For a second Breakfast: any fruit (you can have an orange or Apple). Lunch consists of boiled potatoes with one piece of chicken.
  • The midday meal consists of a vegetable salad with olive oil.
  • Dinner consists of baked fish and tomato.

Advantages of this diet:

1) no stress for the body;

2) savings, since this diet it does not require the mandatory maintenance of expensive products.

Contraindications:

Pregnant women, and if there are problems with the kidneys, such a diet is not suitable as a large volume of drinking liquid strongly loads them. At the same time, your health may deteriorate.

The article is exploratory in nature. The diet itself has contraindications, which it is best to familiarize yourself with before starting the diet.
