How much weight should a woman have in 50 years (to be healthy)

Good afternoon, slimmers!

How much weight do you think a woman should have in her 50s? Moreover, not just the weight on the scales, but the weight that allows a person to be healthy.

As a rule, at the age of 50, a woman enters menopause (menopause). And on 50-year-old age is imposed by a hormonal adjustment. Due to this, the fat layer in a woman increases.

So what weight should be normal for a woman?

Let's turn to who (world health organization) and read what is overweight or obese obesity.

According to who, the diagnosis of "overweight" or" obese " in adults is made in the following cases:
BMI greater than or equal to 25 — overweight;
BMI greater than or equal to 30 is obese.

What is BMI?

This is the body mass index human's.

To calculate it, you need to divide a person's weight (in kg) by their height (in meters, and squared).

For example, your weight is 60 kg. A height of 1.7 m.

So we divide 60/1, 7*1,7= 22,5

If this figure was more than 25, then this is excess weight.

If this indicator exceeds the 30 mark, then this is already obese.

Therefore, every woman at the age of 50 can determine whether her weight norm is 50 years old or not. Simply determine your BMI using the formula.

I started losing weight from a weight of 85 kg, and this was not just overweight for me, but obese. So, you need to watch your weight. otherwise obese it will develop into a second degree, and so on.

What is the danger of obesity, perhaps you all know? These include diabetes mellitus, stroke, heart attack, and cardiovascular diseases.

The conclusion is that a fat person, or even someone who is overweight, cannot be healthy. Since excess weight (read " fat") already associated with a violation metabolism.

Women, be healthy!
